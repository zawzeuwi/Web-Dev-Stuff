Clubs Directory

Tech Innovators Club

The Tech Innovators Club is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering creativity and innovation in technology. The club provides a platform for students interested in coding, robotics, AI, and app development to collaborate on projects, attend workshops, and participate in hackathons. The club aims to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and real-world tech applications.

Green Earth Society

The Green Earth Society is focused on environmental awareness, sustainability, and community outreach. The club organizes tree planting drives, clean-up campaigns, recycling workshops, and eco-awareness seminars. Its mission is to promote a greener campus and encourage responsible environmental behavior among students and staff.

Literary & Debating Club

The Literary & Debating Club is the hub for students passionate about literature, writing, and public speaking. The club hosts debates, open mics, poetry slams, writing contests, and reading sessions. It encourages critical thinking, confident communication, and creative expression among its members.
